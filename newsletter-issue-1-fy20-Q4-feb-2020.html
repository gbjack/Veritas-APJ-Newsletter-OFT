Message From Gary
Hello everyone and welcome to the inaugural issue of our APJ Channel News. The Channel business is vital to the success of Veritas globally, and this is especially true in Asia Pacific & Japan. I hope that you all understand the improvements we have made in our business, and this newsletter will give you all a wider understanding how each of our 6 Regions are winning in their local markets. In summary, this publication is intended for you, so please let us know what you would like to see in the future.
 
We will publish this quarterly and we intend to bring you the latest news and updates of our previous quarter, opportunities for our current and future quarters, along with my expectations and focus areas for all of us in the APJ Channel & Alliances Team.
 
In this issue you will hear from each of our Regional Channel Leaders on how you have been successful in FY20, and how they are planning our future success in FY21.
 
As I look back on FY20, I am very proud of the progress we have made.
 
• We have seen significant investments by the company in our Channel business which has allowed us to recruit the best talent to the APJ Channel & Alliances Team.
 
• You have increased the percentage of the APJ business going through our Partners YoY.
 
• You have significantly grown our top Partners and our business going through Distribution.
 
• You have dramatically improved our Channel business with Opportunity Registration, enabling us to better assess how our Channel Partners are using VPDF and measuring the impact to our business.
 
• We have increased our focus on our Commercial Products, the Backup Exec family.
 
• We have also introduced a new process to measure and communicate the value of the Channel to Veritas in APJ. With the classification of Channel Discrete and Channel Led, we all can now recognise the value of our Partner relationships.
 
And for all of this, I thank you.
 
• Improved economics and benefits to our Platinum Partners who exceed our expectations and accelerate their growth with Veritas.
 
• Continual improvement in our coverage model to focus our efforts on the right Partners.
 
• Further expansion of Partner Led business through continual focus on Opportunity Registration.
 
• Continual improvement in our Go‐To‐Market model to non-named customers.
 
The outbreak of COVID‐19 has presented challenges for all of us and I urge each of you to please remain safe and take whatever precautions you deem fit. I look forward to seeing each of you in person when it is safe to do so. Please take care of yourself, your families, and each other, as we proactively make our way together through these challenging times.
 
Finally, I wish each and every one of you my very best wishes in closing out FY20 and building on your success to achieve greater things in FY21. Go Team!!!

Asia South Region
 
ASR had an excellent Q3, with the Channel business contributing 49% of the total ASR performance and +20% YoY growth. This was made possible with two multi‑million dollar wins, a Channel Enablement Program focusing on 4 key areas to grow our partner ecosystem, BE Elite Partner Program targeting partners who are committed to jointly work with Veritas to achieve our FY21 BE growth goals, and the newly established ASR Inside Sales team to drive the BE business across the 5 countries. Last but not least, a warm welcome to Jayda Lam, newly appointed ASR Alliance and Strategic Managed Partner Manager.
 
 
Suresh Nair
ASR Channel Leader
 
Lily Mei
GCR Channel Leader
Great China Region
 
Although China’s business has been impacted by external factors, GCR’s FY20 Q3 Channel business still reflected positive growth. This is due to the successes from Platinum and Gold Partners, Unmanaged Partner business, and BE business. The team has identified a number of focus areas they would like to drive in FY21 to keep the momentum and grow their business even further. Check out the China Partner Eco WeChat social media app – to energize partners, drive demand generation and amplify the messages the team wants to promote to our customers and partners in Veritas Voice. We are still facing the threat of COVID‐19, and GCR will continue to experience very tough times. With the support from our partners, we will continue to work together to overcome our challenges and fight to win this battle.
 
India Region
 
In Q3, India Channel team achieved exceptional growth in the Mid‑Market and Commercial segment – a big thanks to the PTAM for working with partners to drive this 100% incremental revenue which has contributed close to 20% revenue for the geo. 2 key factors, the ISO team and the Channel incentive program, also contributed to Q3’s growth, by supporting our partners and distributors to drive incremental revenue. The team has been instrumental in expanding its reach and winning against competition, securing a number of wins across the region – Parle Industries, Volkswagen and PSA Groupe (India), ROBI BFSI (Bangladesh) and Dialog (Sri Lanka).
 
 
Sharad Gupta
INR Channel Leader
 
Mitsuru Shijo
JPR Channel Leader
Japan Region
 
Japan Channel fell slightly short of our Q3 All‑Up plan, closing at 85.3% of plan, with a +4.7% YoY growth (vs 100% plan, +7.9% YoY growth). For BE, we closed at 75% of plan, with +14.2% YoY growth vs our 92.5% plan +8.8% YoY growth. Due to the influence of Covid‑19, Q4 is in a severe situation. In the remaining weeks, we will do our best to reach YoY Plus throughout Q4 and the year.
 
Korea Region
 
In FY20, the Korea team’s key Channel strategies are to set up a value‐added distribution business, as well as to generate incremental revenue with our major resellers and Alliance partners. Their Q3 YTD results has validated these strategies – USD 906K in bookings generated by 39 new partners, USD 1.6M bookings from strategic growth initiative by major partners, USD 195K with our Alliance initiatives, etc. The team has identified 3 key focus areas to drive further growth in FY21.
 
 
Lee Kwang Won
KOR Channel Leader
 
Nigel Youlden

Pacific Region
 
It’s been a defining and challenging year for the Pacific Region. The Channel team, all with less than a year tenure, has pulled together; committed to rebuilding our business in region. Q3 was a pivotal point in time and we came together as a leadership team and course‑corrected to align resources to Partners with jointly aligned growth aspirations. This is the right strategy for our business.
